GIMP 

open png with gimp
colors > desaturate > desaturate luminance
duplicate layer & select top layer
filters > blur > gaussian blur 2.5-4.5 usually is 4.5
layer mode > Multiply
merge down
remove any unwanted artifacts in the design by painting white
invert logo such that we export a white png
if necessary, color > levels > drag top bar as far right as needed to blackout the logo
fuzzy select tool > thresland 32.5
click the logo to select, adjust parameters as necessary to get a clean selection. the white and black contrast is essential. 
new layer & select it
selection > sharpen (fills in holes)
paint bucket white
hide first layer
export to png
